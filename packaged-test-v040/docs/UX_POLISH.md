### Review desktop zoom-out behaviour
- Context: Current zoom uses cursor/finger-centred zoom for both zooming in and zooming out.
- Observation: On desktop mouse wheel, zooming out may feel slightly unnatural because the view pulls away from the cursor point rather than feeling centred on the map.
- Status: Deferred
- Notes: Revisit after pan, camera buttons, fit map, reset view, and minimap are implemented.
